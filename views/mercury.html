Welcome to Mercury

  • Mercury is the smallest planet in the solar system.
  • It is the closest planet to the sun.
  • It is a terrestial planet, comprised of minerals and metals.
  • Mercury has no satellites.
  • Because of its lack of a significant atmosphere, temperatures on the planet range between very hot and cold.
  • Though it is the closest planet to the sun, it is not the hottest.
