Welcome to Venus

  • Venus is the sixth-largest planet in the solar system.
  • It is the second-closest planet to the sun.
  • It is a terrestial planet, comprised of minerals and metals.
  • Venus has no satellites.
  • It is the hottest planet in the solar system, reaching temperatures of up to 900 degrees Fahrenheit.
  • Venus is visible from Earth, and is the second brightest object in the sky after the Sun and Moon.
