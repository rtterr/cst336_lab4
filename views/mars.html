Welcome to Mars

  • Mars is the second-smallest planet in the solar system.
  • It is the fourth-closest planet to the sun.
  • It is a terrestial planet, comprised of minerals and metals. It is the largest of such planets.
  • Mars has two known satellites, Phobos and Deimos.
  • Water exists within ice in the dirt and in thin clouds.
  • Mars has an atosphere, albeit a rather thin one, comprised of carbon dioxide, nitrogen, and argon.
